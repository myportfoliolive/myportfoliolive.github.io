About Me

I’m Aayan Wasti, a Grade 11 student at White Oaks Secondary School, and a passionate learner in the fields of programming and artificial intelligence. My journey began with a curiosity to understand how technology shapes the world. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in web development, AI, and UI/UX design.

As the CEO & Founder of AIHealth, I developed HealthBot, an AI-powered chatbot aimed at improving access to health information. My goal is to create technology that not only simplifies lives but also makes a meaningful impact on society.

Beyond coding, I enjoy mentoring peers, exploring innovative projects, and staying on the cutting edge of tech trends. When I’m not working on my next big idea, you’ll find me learning about emerging AI technologies or brainstorming startup concepts.
